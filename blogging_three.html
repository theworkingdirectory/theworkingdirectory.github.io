On blogging (vol. III)

I think it’s been about a year since I started this blog. Here’s just a very brief update on why I’m doing this.

I’ve given up on actually becoming a good writer, or rather, I’ve lost interest in trying. I’m still working on eliminating my most insufferable stylistic indulgences, but I’m generally okay with being a fluent user of the English language who occasionally puts things in a slightly interesting way. I realized that the main appeal of this blog for me lies elsewhere. I’m wildly allergic to people showing off their intelligence or education or anything else for that matter, and whenever I catch myself doing it I cringe in a way that is physically painful. The nice thing about having an extremely private blog is that I can just write about what interests me without having to worry that I might be trying to impress anyone.
